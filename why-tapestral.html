How Vendors Benefit

Vendors don’t have to run another storefront or manage new systems. Instead, they provide their product catalog to Tapestral, and we handle everything else. From presenting products to designers, to processing payments, to communicating order updates — we ensure vendors can focus on fulfillment while gaining access to a growing network of designers.

What We Handle for Vendors

  • Importing vendor product data into Tapestral
  • Showcasing products directly to designers

  • Receiving and recording vendor order acknowledgements

Our Role

Tapestral sits in the middle — connecting designers who need reliable sourcing with vendors who want to reach them. We operate the platform, manage the technology, and ensure both sides experience a smoother, faster process. Designers see clarity. Vendors see sales. We handle the complexity in between.

What Powers It All

  • Purpose-built platform — not a template or site builder
  • Fast, scalable infrastructure for smooth browsing and ordering
  • Real-time updates on inventory, lead times, and order statuses
  • End-to-end encryption and secure payment processing
  • Automated order pipelines to reduce delays and errors

Why It Matters

Designers gain a simpler way to source and order. Vendors gain a direct path to sales without added overhead. And because Tapestral manages the process, both sides benefit from faster projects, fewer mistakes, and a platform built specifically for the furniture industry.
