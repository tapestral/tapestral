About Tapestral

Elevating design. Simplifying sourcing.

Our Mission

Tapestral is built to empower interior designers by streamlining access to curated, high-quality furniture direct from manufacturers. We eliminate friction in sourcing, quoting, and order management — so you can stay focused on delivering exceptional spaces.

What We Offer

  • Direct-to-designer access to vetted manufacturers
  • The Gallery, organize and present curated selections
  • Transparent order tracking and downloadable documents
  • Simple pricing, clear terms, and designer-focused service
